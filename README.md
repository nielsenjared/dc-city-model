# Washington, DC 3D City Model

![Alt Text](/images/jared-nielsen-washington-dc-city-model-01.png "Washington, DC City Model")

In a collaboration with [Two Bit Circus](http://twobitcircus.com/), my brother and I designed, fabricated and installed a scale model, artistic interpretation of the future of Washington, DC featuring 3D printed buildings and vehicles in the newly opened [LocalMotors storefront in National Harbor, MD](https://www.dclibrary.org/labs/fablab).

[Watch the time-lapse build](https://vimeo.com/222075807)
